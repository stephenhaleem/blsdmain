MEET
SETITUN OLAWUYI

Realtor, Builder & Videographer

Before real estate, I spent over a decade in engineering and construction, learning how homes are truly built — from the foundation to the smallest hidden details. I’ve been on job sites, reviewed plans, and caught defects that could have cost families tens of thousands of dollars. That experience gave me something most agents don’t have: the ability to see past the finishes and protect what really matters.

Today, I don’t just help people buy and sell homes;I help them feel safe, informed, and confident in the biggest decision of their lives. I lead with honesty, I speak up when something feels wrong, and I treat every client like family. This isn’t just business to me. It’s personal.

f you want to work with someone who understands both the science of buildings and the emotion of buying a home, I’d be honored to help you take that next step, with clarity, protection, and heart.

What I Specialize In:

  • Pre-construction opportunities
  • New builds & resale homes
  • Investment properties
  • Construction-quality analysis
  • High-end video marketing for listings

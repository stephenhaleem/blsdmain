Meet Me

Hi, I'm Setitun (Blessing) Olawuyi.

Born in Lagos, Nigeria, I moved to London, Ontario at age 12, and later pursued studies in Kinesiology, Civil Engineering, and Construction Management. With over 13 years of experience managing million dollar projects, I bring a sharp eye for detail and a results-driven mindset to every real estate transaction.

Based in Calgary, I specialize in new constructions, presales, and real estate investments, guiding clients to properties that fit their goals perfectly. As a self-taught videographer and photographer, I also run a media company, creating compelling visual stories that help businesses and individuals shine.

My mission is simple: to empower you with knowledge, transparency, and exceptional service, ensuring your real estate journey and every project we work on together exceeds expectations.

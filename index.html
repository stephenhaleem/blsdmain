Meet Me

Hi, I'm Setitun (Blessing) Olawuyi.

Born in Lagos, Nigeria, I moved to London, Ontario at age 12, and later pursued studies in Kinesiology, Civil Engineering, and Construction Management. With over 13 years of experience managing million dollar projects, I bring a sharp eye for detail and a results-driven mindset to every real estate transaction.

Based in Calgary, I specialize in new constructions, presales, and real estate investments, guiding clients to properties that fit their goals perfectly. As a self-taught videographer and photographer, I also run a media company, creating compelling visual stories that help businesses and individuals shine.

My mission is simple: to empower you with knowledge, transparency, and exceptional service, ensuring your real estate journey and every project we work on together exceeds expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about buying, selling, and investing in Calgary real estate

What are the current market conditions in Calgary?

Calgary's real estate market is dynamic with strong demand across all quadrants (SW, SE, NW, NE). New construction and presale properties are particularly popular. We provide current market analysis and insights to help you make informed decisions. Contact us for a detailed market report tailored to your specific area of interest.

How do I access off-market properties?

Join our VIP Waitlist above to receive exclusive access to off-market properties before they're publicly listed. These opportunities often offer better pricing and less competition. As a VIP member, you'll receive weekly updates on the best deals matching your criteria.

What's included in your property videography service?

Our professional videography service includes cinematic property tours, drone footage (where permitted), professional editing, and social media-optimized versions. This gives your listing maximum exposure and helps it stand out in Calgary's competitive market.

Do you work with first-time home buyers?

Absolutely! We specialize in guiding first-time buyers through every step of the process. From understanding financing options and mortgage pre-approval to finding the perfect home and negotiating the best price, we're with you every step of the way.

What areas of Calgary do you serve?

We serve all quadrants of Calgary including SW, SE, NW, and NE, as well as surrounding areas. Whether you're interested in established neighborhoods, new developments, or investment properties, we have extensive knowledge across all Calgary communities.

How can I get a free home valuation?

Getting a free, no-obligation home valuation is easy! Simply fill out the contact form above and select "Free Home Valuation" as your subject. We'll provide a comprehensive market analysis of your property's current value based on recent comparable sales and current market conditions.
